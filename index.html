Facts vs. opinions:
  • Facts (assumptions): Fixed average speeds and straight-line distances. 1 AU ≈ 149,597,870.7 km; 1 ly ≈ 9.4607×10^12 km; 1 pc ≈ 3.0857×10^13 km; c ≈ 299,792.458 km/s.
  • Opinions: What counts as “conventional” is debatable; historic aircraft included for fun.
